Tribute to YS Jagan Mohan Reddy

A visionary leader and architect of change, YS Jagan Mohan Reddy made history in the 2019 Andhra Pradesh elections, winning 151 seats.

Political Journey

  • 2004: Entered politics and actively supported his father, Dr. YS Rajasekhara Reddy (YSR), during his tenure as Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh.
  • 2009: After the untimely demise of YSR, Jagan began his independent political journey by founding the YSR Congress Party.
  • 2011: Officially launched the YSR Congress Party, pledging to continue his father’s legacy and serve the people of Andhra Pradesh.
  • 2012: Emerged as a strong opposition leader in the state, advocating for the welfare of farmers, youth, and marginalized communities.
  • 2019: Achieved a landslide victory in the Andhra Pradesh Assembly elections, winning 151 out of 175 seats and becoming the Chief Minister.

YS Jagan's journey is a testament to his resilience, dedication, and vision for a better future for Andhra Pradesh. From walking thousands of kilometers during his Padayatra to addressing the issues of millions, he has proven himself as a people's leader.

"Leadership is not about power; it's about service to the people."

– YS Jagan Mohan Reddy
